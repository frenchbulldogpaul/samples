About myself

My family

The city of Athens became my new home. My new family immediately fell in love with me and spoiled me a lot. I like to spend time together. I love long walks and meeting other dogs.

On my first birthday, they gave me a surprise and had a meat cake that I ate quickly.I have a very good talent - sad, hungry eyes. When I look with my sad eyes, people always share their food with me. Of course, then my mother scolds me and says that this is not allowed, but sometimes she breaks her word and shares sweets with me.
